Getting Started

Follow the below steps to get started to index your website...

  • 1Account Creation

    If you have not created an account yet with Svolve, please Sign Up for an account now.

  • 2Add a website

    Login to the account created in Step 1. Add a website to your account. Every website has its own token/id. This will be a unique identifier which will be used to search/query your website/domain.

  • 3Submit the site for Crawling

    Once you add your website and when you are ready, submit your website for crawling by the Svolve crawler.

  • 4Integrate your website search with Svolve - Site search

    The integration of websites with sitesearch has been made very simple. A simple HTTP call is all that is required. Read More.
